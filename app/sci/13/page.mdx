### Topics *(Pick 1)*
> We learned about waves, digital communication, and digital information storage.
> 
> Pick something that's relevant to your life


#### General Wave Behavior^collapsed
- Different mediums' effects on waves
- The Doppler effect: where it gets in our way, how it's useful to us
- How light wave polarization works & how we use it
- Diffraction gratings & what it's useful for
- What interference is, when it gets in our way, how we overcome it, and how we use it

#### Light/Electromagnetic Waves^collapsed
- Develop a light communication system
- How radio waves work
- How different materials affect Wi-Fi signal strength
- Polarization of light and create polarized light filters
- Interference in wave transmission & how we overcome it
- How camera sensors work

#### Sound Waves^collapsed
- The physics of musical instruments
- Frequencies of different music scales
- How noise-cancelling headphones work
- What photo-acoustic imaging is and how it works
- Acoustic design in performance halls
- Types of soundproofing and how they work
- Speaker cabinet design
- How music is mixed and mastered

#### Medicinal Radiation & Information^collapsed
- How ultrasound imaging works
- How magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) works
- X-Ray machines
- Cancer radiation therapy
- HIPAA privacy laws

#### Digital Data^collapsed
- Your digital footprint
- The role of metadata in digital media
- Data privacy and rights
- Social media algorithms
- What cookies are and how they're being replaced with (Google Privacy Sandbox)
- Effects of data encryption on transmission speed and security
- What a secure sockets layer (SSL) is and how it keeps your data secure
- How public key infrastructure (PKI) keeps our communication secure
- How digital rights management (DRM) secures content
- Privacy & safety in online video games
- Ownership, copyright, and how we track who owns content

#### Technology Stuff^collapsed
- Compression algorithms
- Wireless communication in space
- How NFC (near-field communication) works
- Effects of different encoding schemes on digital data transmission
- How QR codes work, even when scratched
- Error correction in digital data transmission
- Quantum computers and how they threaten modern cybersecurity
- How fiber optic sensors & communication work

#### Astronomy^collapsed
- Redshift & secondaryshift: how they work and what we learn from them
- Radio telescopes: how they work and what we can see with them
- Gravitational waves: what they are and what we learn from them
- The Hubble Space Telescope: why it needed to be fixed as soon as it went to space
- The James Webb Space Telescope: why it's so unique/special/cool
- Light spectra & how they show us what stars are made of
- Light Pollution
